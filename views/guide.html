Welcome, here is your website user guide!
To start with, you can:

  • Go to 'My Account' at navigation bar to create or join a family.

  • Go to 'Upload Profiles' at navigation bar to create a profile for you and others in your family

  • To change your username or switch current family, you can go to 'My Account' Page

  • 'Home', 'Artifacts' and 'Family' pages only show information of your current family

  • To invite people to a family, you need to let them know the family's id and password, which are held by the family's creator

  • You can go back to check out this page anytime when clicking 'User Guide' on navigation bar

  • If you have any advice to this website, please contact us at mystery_it@gmail.com
